Succulents

This type of succulent is called a Hen & Chick, the bigger flower acts as the hen and reproduces smaller flowers known as chicks. The coolest part about these is that you can simply pull a baby chick out, repot it, and it will continue to grow and reproduce more chicks. These are some of my favorite plants, they require a good amount of sun but only need to be watered once a week if that. They're not only super easy to maintain, but super affordable!

These are all succulents that I have bought and replanted. I no longer remember the names of each individual type, but in the right of the pot you can see another type of Hen & Chick as mentioned above. Buying and repotting is super fun, as you can see I added a statue and pebbles to add some personality. (Check out my Greensboro Page for information on where these were found!)
